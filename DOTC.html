DEPTHS OF THE CAVE

Depths of the Cave (often styled as "DEPTHS OF THE CAVE" or acronymed to DOTC) is my first released, being a 1-track rock single. It is sourced directly from the OST of a scrapped Disitus show made in Dreams, with most tracks from it being remixes of tracks from Disitus 2 and 1.

As for the rest of that cancelled show's OST, if you're interested, is probably lost, but I bet I could potentially dig some of it up and try to extract it. Although exporting Dreams songs is extremely tedious as the only way I know of to do it is to record it and then export the video onto a flash drive and convert it to an audio file, which means I can't get lossless and the audio is probably degraded a bit from the recording.

  • Release date: unknown, likely around September 2023
  • Platforms: YouTube Music, Spotify, Apple Music, and more
